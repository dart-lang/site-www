## Usage

To enable the `{{lint.name}}` rule,
add `{{lint.name}}` under **linter > rules** in your
[`analysis_options.yaml`](/tools/analysis) file:

```yaml title="analysis_options.yaml"
linter:
  rules:
    - {{lint.name}}
```
